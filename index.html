32 Panels Design

It has a traditional 32 panel design for impact distribution, stable flight and predictable ball movement.

Thread Winded Bladder

SR Bladder is used in the core of the football for optimal shape retention and bounce. It provides a steady performance as it is less prone to variations. The bladder is winded to keep the football intact and dent-free.

130g PC Fabric

PC 130 fabric is used as the lining for a light-weight feel and steady performance.

3.0 mm Eva Foam Sheet

The inner layer is EVA foam padding for a more comfortable playing experience and to reduce the risk of injuries.

.15 MM TPU Sheet

The outer cover is constructed from TPU material with a shine finish which allows for a consistent performance and contributes to the longevity of the ball.
